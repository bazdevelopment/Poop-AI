Poop AI – Data & Account Deletion Policy

This page explains how users of the Poop AI app can request deletion of their account and personal data. Poop AI is published on Google Play by BAZEUS / Fitness AI Coach Tracker-EXFIT .

In-App Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account directly within the Poop AI app:

  • Open the Poop AI app
  • Go to Settings > Deactivate & Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion – this will remove your account and associated data
